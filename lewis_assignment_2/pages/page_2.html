Advantages of Using Audio and Video

Let us start out with some of the advantages of using audio and video on your web site. When using audio and video in your web site it gives your site more appeal, which draws in the attention of the user. The purpose of your web site is to draw in users and keep their attention to stay on your site. Another major advantage of using audio and video on your web site is you can use it for educational videos about your company, instructional videos, or even introductions or messages from and/or to the staff members.
