Disadvantages of Using Audio and Video

Now for some disadvantages of using audio and video in your web site. I believe that the biggest disadvantage of using audio and video in your web site is the amount of time that it can take to load it when going on to the site. There is nothing worse than to have to wait around for something to load up because of the size of the file. Another disadvantage of using audio and video in your web site could be the time and cost of making the video if you want a professional looking video.
