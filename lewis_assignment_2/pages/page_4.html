My Web Site Details

Now let's take a look at the audio and video used throughout my site. The audio and video that I used throughout my site was downloaded from the Pixabay web site. It is very important not to use any copyrighted material on your web site. On my home page I used a video for the background of a skeleton dancing, and with this I added an audio file of some music. I made both of these media choices on an autoplay, so the first thing that you see when you open up my site's home page you will see a skeleton dancing to the music, I think it is very entertaining, and will catch the users attention. The next four pages I used videos with controls. I added audio files that I think went well with the videos. I used Adobe Audition to edit the audio to the length of the video with a fade at the end to give it some pizzaz.
