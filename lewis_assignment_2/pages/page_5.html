The Closing

In closing, I would like to say how enjoyable this project was to work with. This project made me really think about how everything between the audio and video would all tie together, and to find the right audio to match up with the videos. Using audio and video will be a big part of building web sites in the future, and this gave me some good experience working with both types of media. Although there were a few things that seemed to not function correctly at first, I was able to figure it all out, and I believe that this assignment turned out pretty well.
