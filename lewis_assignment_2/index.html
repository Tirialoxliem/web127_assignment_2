Advantages and Disadvantages of Using Audio and Video


Using audio and video in web sites is a necessary item to use. Without using audio and video your web site could be pretty dull. There are different ways of providing audio and video to your web site, such as embedded right on the screen in the site with controls, or autoplay without controls. You could have an individual video with no audio, you could have audio with no video, or you can have them both together. On these next few pages I will dive deeper into some of the advantages and disadvantages of using audio and video on your web site.
